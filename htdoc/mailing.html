There are now several mailing lists related to ht://Dig.

htdig@htdig.org is a relatively high volume list for discussion of ht://Dig and related announcements. Major announcements, such as new releases are sent to this list as well as the htdig3-announce list below.
htdig3-announce@htdig.org is a read-only list only for announcements of ht://Dig releases from the main htdig list.
htdig3-dev@htdig.org is a fairly moderate volume list for discussion of ht://Dig development issues and patches.
htdig3-updates@htdig.org is a read-only, relatively high volume list that tracks changes to the CVS source tree changes.
